TACO BELL

Taco Bell is an American fast food restaurant that consist of Tex-Mex style foods. It was founded in Downey, California, and its current headquarters is in Irvine, California. Taco Bell has been around since 1962 (56 years ago) and is still one of the most popular fast food restaurants in America. As of 2015, there are over 7,000 locations including several out of the country. There are locations in Asia, Europe, the Middle East, North America, Oceania, South America, and other countries. Taco Bell also has a line of clothing consisting of hoodies, jackets, shirts, and even body suits.
Taco Bell has a wide variety of food items including burritos, tacos, quesadillas, and nachos. There are several Value Menu items including mini quesadillas, burritos, and even some deserts. Some of the fan favorites are the chalupas, the recently introduced Doritos Locos Tacos, and the burrito supreme. There are also many discontinued menu items including The Bell Beefer, seafood salad, Nacho Crunch Grilled Stuft Burrito, Grilled Stuft Nacho, and much more.
